  • BEST MANAGER

    Best Manager is a collection of mind-boggling issues that a manager is likely to confront in the business world.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. Participants should adhere to the institute's code of conduct and discipline.
    2. Any malpractice or misbehaviour towards volunteers or judges will lead to disqualification and serious actions would be taken against the individual.
    3. All participants must have a laptop with a working camera, microphone and a stable internet connection.
    4. One individual can participate in only one event.
    5. All teams should have the required number of members as per the event specifications.
    6. All members of a team are expected to be present for every round on time, the absence of any member will lead to the disqualification of the team.
    7. When a team or a team member is requested to be on camera, please ensure that your face, hands and notepads (not what you are writing of course) are clearly visible.
    8. Participants are requested to be in semi-formal or formal attire.
    9. Make sure you save event POC’s contact information throughout the event.
    10. Any doubts regarding the event should be cleared prior to the commencement of the event.
    11. In case of any emergency, please let the POCs know as soon as possible.
    12. The decision of the volunteers and judges will be final.
    13. The committee has the right to change the time and venue of the events.
    14. There would be no refund provided if the participants back out.

    For more details refer to the rulebook and brochure in homepage.
