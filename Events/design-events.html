  • GLIMPSE INTO THE UNFOCUSED - Online Photography event

    We are challenging you to express your opinions and voice it using the medium of photography.We are looking for a piece that is creative,original and may even be thought-provoking or out of the box that inspires artists all around the world.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. Only one entry per individual is allowed.
    2. Image data file format should be jpeg & File size should not be more than 4MB.
    4. A short description of 50 - 75 words or less about the photograph is mandatory.
    5. There are a total of two rounds - the first round and the final second round, after which the Top three winners and 10 special mentions will be announced.
    6. The shutter speed,ISO,Aperture and camera make should be mentioned.
    7. Edited photographs will be disqualified.
    8. Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.

    For more details refer to the rulebook and brochure in homepage.

    Mode of conduction : Online platform

    Team Size: Individual

  • REVIVAL OUTLOOK - OUTFIT DESIGN EVENT

    An outfit designing competition that aims to shed light on traditional handicrafts representing the textile and craft community of India and reviving vulnerable crafts through elegantly designed attires and customized accessories.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. The materials and outfits for the event should be pre-planned and brought by the participants.
    2. Ready-to-use clothing apparels not entertained.
    3. Priority will be given to the usage of sustainable/ recyclable materials.
    4. Clothes should not be revealing in any form.
    5. The performers must have a full body cloth underneath their designed outfits if opting for a Sleeveless or above-knee length outfits.
    6. The decision of the jury panel will be final.
    7. Plagiarism strictly not acceptable.

    For more details refer to the rulebook and brochure in homepage.

    Mode of conduction : Offline (At Christ University- Kengeri campus)

    Team Size: 4-8 members

  • TerrAbstract - CLAY MODELLING EVENT

    Get your hands dirty in the process of sculpting. Play & quarrel clay in order to create an abstract sculpture. Channel your inner creativity to produce an artwork while having fun at the same time.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. Topic for abstraction will be given on spot.
    2. Participants have to make an abstract clay model
    3. The model has to be made within the given amount of clay.
    4. TIME ALLOTTED: 3 hours
